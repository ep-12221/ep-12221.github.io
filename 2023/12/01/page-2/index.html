Dn​=∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣​2cosx10⋮00​12cosx1⋮00​012cosx⋮00​……………​000⋮2cosx1​000⋮12cosx​∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣​.

2.计算n阶行列式

Dn=∣11…1xx…xx2x2…x2⋮⋮⋮xn−2xn−2…xn−2xnxn…xn∣.D_n = \left |\begin{array}{cccc} 1 &1 & \dots &1\\ x &x &\dots&x\\ x^2 &x^2&\dots &x^2\\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ x^{n-2} &x^{n-2}&\dots &x^{n-2}\\ x^n &x^n & \dots &x^n\\ \end{array}\right|. ∣11…11xx…xyx2x2…x2y2⋮⋮⋮⋮xn−2xn−2…xn−2yn−2xn−1xn−1…xn−1yn−1xnxn…xnyn∣=∏k=1n(y−xk)∏1≤j<i≤n(xi−xj)\left |\begin{array}{cccc} 1 &1 & \dots &1&1\\ x &x &\dots&x&y\\ x^2 &x^2&\dots &x^2&y^2\\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots &\vdots\\ x^{n-2} &x^{n-2}&\dots &x^{n-2}&y^{n-2}\\ x^{n-1} &x^{n-1}&\dots &x^{n-1}&y^{n-1}\\ x^n &x^n & \dots &x^n&y^n\\ \end{array}\right|=\prod \limits_{k=1}^n(y-x_k)\prod_{1\le j<i\le n}(x_i-x_j) ∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣​1xx2⋮xn−2xn−1xn​1xx2⋮xn−2xn−1xn​………………​1xx2⋮xn−2xn−1xn​1yy2⋮yn−2yn−1yn​∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣∣​=k=1∏n​(y−xk​)1≤j<i≤n∏​(xi​−xj​)

Dn+1=A1,n+1+yA2,n+1+⋯+yn−1An,n+1+ynAn+1,n+1D_{n+1} = A_{1,n+1} + yA_{2,n+1}+\dots +y^{n-1}A_{n,n+1}+y^nA_{n+1,n+1} Dn+1​=A1,n+1​+yA2,n+1​+⋯+yn−1An,n+1​+ynAn+1,n+1​

∏k=1n(y−xk)=yn−yn−1∑k=1nxk+⋯+(−1)n∏k=1nxk\prod \limits_{k=1}^n(y-x_k) = y^n-y^{n-1}\sum_{k=1}^{n}x_k+\dots +(-1)^n\prod_{k=1}^{n}x_k k=1∏n​(y−xk​)=yn−yn−1k=1∑n​xk​+⋯+(−1)nk=1∏n​xk​

B.证明题

设{αi}\{\alpha _i\}{αi​}为n−1n-1n−1个线性无关的nnn维向量组，ξ1,ξ2\xi _1,\xi_2ξ1​,ξ2​为与该向量组向量均正交的两个nnn维列向量，证明：ξ1,ξ2\xi _1,\xi_2ξ1​,ξ2​线性相关。

证明：将 $\xi _1 $ 添入向量组中，考虑到正交即线性无关，包含 ξ1\xi _1ξ1​ 的向量组即为空间的一组基，并设 ξ1\xi_1ξ1​ 在该组基下的坐标表示为(k1,k2,…kn)(k_1,k_2,\dots k_n)(k1​,k2​,…kn​)即有

ξ1Tξ2=(∑i=1n−1kiαiT+knξ2T)ξ2=knξ2Tξ2\xi_1 ^T\xi _2 =(\sum_{i=1}^{n-1}k_i\alpha_i^T+k_n\xi_2^T)\xi _2=k_n\xi_2^T\xi_2 ξ1T​ξ2​=(i=1∑n−1​ki​αiT​+kn​ξ2T​)ξ2​=kn​ξ2T​ξ2​
